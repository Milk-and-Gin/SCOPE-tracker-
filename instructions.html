How the SCOPE Equation Works

This guide explains each field in the tracker and how it contributes to magical efficacy (Ψ) and probability shift (P*).

The Equation Overview

All factors combine multiplicatively to produce Ψ (efficacy), which then modifies the baseline probability P into P* (Enchanted Probability).

Ψ (efficacy)
κ (calibration) × Internal Alignment (H) × External Engagement (X)
(A_focus × B_belief × (1–D_attach) × (1–R_res)) (S_skill × L_link × R_repr × C_coord)
× Vitality (V) × Noise (Q)

Then:

P* = P + (1 – P) × Ψ

Meta Information

Date, Location, Tradition — Context for your ritual.

Goal Statement — A clear description of your intent in the present tense.

Reference Class (Baseline) — The comparable situation without magical action.

Baseline Probability P — Numeric estimate (0–1) of how likely the event is naturally.

Inputs

Attention (A_focus) — How focused and absorbed you are in the intention.

Belief (B_belief) — Your confidence in the operation’s success.

Attachment (D_attach) — Excess desire or “lust of result.” Tracker uses 1 – D_attach.

Resistance (R_res) — Inner doubt or conflict. Tracker uses 1 – R_res.

Skill (S_skill) — Your ritual proficiency.

Link (L_link) — The strength of the sympathetic/magical connection.

Representation (R_repr) — Clarity and resonance of your symbols or talismans.

Coordination (C_coord) — Synchrony of group action if applicable.

Vitality (V) — Your physical and mental energy.

Noise (Q) — Internal and external distractions reducing efficacy.

κ (kappa) — A calibration constant unique to you.

Outputs

Ψ (efficacy) — Combined score representing the strength of magical alignment.

P* (Enchanted Probability) — The updated probability of success after magical influence. If Ψ = 0, nothing changes. If Ψ > 0, P* increases but never exceeds 1.
