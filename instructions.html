How the SCOPE Equation Works

This guide explains each field in the tracker and how it contributes to the calculation of magical efficacy and probability shift.

Meta Information

Date, Location, Tradition — Context for your ritual. These details help you later spot patterns (e.g. certain places or traditions being more effective).

Goal Statement — A clear description of your intent in the present tense.

Reference Class (Baseline) — The comparable situation without magical action (your control group). For example: “Normally this pitch has a 1 in 3 chance.”

Baseline Probability P — A numeric estimate (0–1) of how likely the event is naturally, before magical influence.

Internal Alignment (H)

Attention (A_focus) — How focused and absorbed you are in the intention. Higher attention = stronger signal.

Belief (B_belief) — Your confidence in the operation’s success. Expectancy effects are powerful amplifiers.

Attachment (D_attach) — The degree of “lust of result.” High attachment harms efficacy. The tracker inverts this (using 1 – D_attach) so that letting go increases effectiveness.

Resistance (R_res) — Inner doubts or conflicts. High resistance reduces outcomes. The tracker uses (1 – R_res), so clearing doubts improves results.

External Engagement (X)

Skill (S_skill) — Your proficiency in the ritual technologies used.

Link (L_link) — How directly your ritual connects to the target (personal items, names, astrological charts, etc.).

Representation (R_repr) — The clarity and resonance of your symbols, sigils, or talismans.

Coordination (C_coord) — The synchrony of group action if working with others (drumming, chanting, movement). High coordination boosts group efficacy.

Supporting Factors

Vitality (V) — Your physical and mental energy. A strong body and rested mind boost concentration and confidence.

Noise (Q) — External and internal distractions that reduce signal strength. Lower noise = clearer signal.

κ (kappa) — A calibration constant unique to you. Start with κ = 1. After logging 15–20 rituals, update it to reflect your observed average uplift.

Outputs

Ψ (efficacy) — This is the combined effectiveness score the tracker calculates. It multiplies all your chosen values, adjusted by κ and noise, to give a single measure of magical “signal strength.”

Enchanted Probability P* — This is the baseline probability P adjusted by Ψ. It represents the new chance of success after magical influence:
P* = P + (1 – P) × Ψ

If Ψ = 0, nothing changes. If Ψ > 0, the probability increases (though it never exceeds 1). This shows how strong alignment across all variables can bend outcomes.
